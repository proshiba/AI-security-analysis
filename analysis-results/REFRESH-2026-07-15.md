## Safety and provenance

- MalwareBazaar was contacted only to query metadata and download password-protected submissions.
- Samples were not executed; real C2, stage, and payload infrastructure was not contacted.
- Recovered layers remain outside the repository in encrypted analysis artifacts.
- Public acquisition manifests remove local `zip_path` values.
- Certificate/OCSP, vendor documentation, external-IP lookup, and public DNS-over-HTTPS references are suppressed as likely false-positive C2 candidates.
